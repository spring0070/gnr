    *   市場正在從單純的「AI 漲勢交易」（Momentum Trade）轉向「AI 分散交易」（Dispersion Trade），這意味著除了基礎設施和硬體供應鏈之外，軟體訂閱和相關產業也將受到關注。

*   **投資與產業前景：軟體生態系的長期價值**
    *   在長期視角下，企業級軟體服務商（Software Vendors）的地位至關重要，由於其產品已經與企業工作流程深度整合，並負責資料治理、系統更新等關鍵環節，因此難以被單一AI技術取代。
    *   大型軟體公司作為戰略夥伴，其業務成長將隨著客戶和產業需求的增長而持續擴展，為長期投資提供了穩固基礎。
    *   此外，公司回購股票的窗口也重新開啟，加上接下來的財報季，預計將持續支撐市場的樂觀情緒。

### English Summary
- The US stock market continues to exhibit strong upward momentum, with major indices like the S&P 500, Nasdaq 100, and Russell 2000 pushing toward record highs.
- The overall market strength shows a "narrow rally" pattern, with gains disproportionately concentrated in a select group of large technology mega-cap stocks.
- The primary economic and market driver is the artificial intelligence (AI) revolution, fueled by sustained and increasing capital expenditure (CapEx) from major cloud service providers (Hyperscalers).
- Economic growth projections suggest that a significant portion of current economic expansion is directly linked to IT and technology CapEx spending.
- Market focus is shifting from a pure "AI momentum trade" (buying only infrastructure/chips) to an "AI dispersion trade," bringing renewed attention to software subscriptions and related industries.
- Corporate earnings season is showing exceptionally strong results, particularly from chip manufacturers and large tech firms, confirming high demand for cloud services.
- Long-term investment value remains robust for enterprise software vendors because their products are deeply integrated into corporate workflows and are crucial for data governance and system updates.
- Major software companies are positioned as strategic partners whose growth will naturally expand alongside their clients' and industries' increasing needs, providing a stable foundation for long-term investment.
- The reopening of the corporate buyback window, combined with the completion of the earnings season, is expected to sustain market optimism.

Tag: S&P 500, Nasdaq 100, Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Services, Large-Cap Tech Stocks

* 市場上輪動焦點集中於大型科技股（Big Tech），資訊科技（IT）類股領漲，年化上漲約 1.4%，成為推動指數創紀錄的主要動力。
* 儘管地緣政治議題（如石油價格）持續波動，國際油價（WTI 和 Brent）本週仍呈現上漲趨勢，雙指標均維持在每桶 100 美元以上，但股市表現反而表現出對這些宏觀風險的暫時性「忽略」。
* 個股動態表現方面，科技與媒體產業股是主要的漲勢來源：
    * **蘋果公司 (Apple)：** 受惠於驚人的第三季度營收預測，股價上漲約 3.3%，其一系列新產品（如 Neo 低成本筆電系列、新 iPad Air 等）持續受到市場追捧。
    * **甲骨文 (Oracle)：** 股價上漲約 6.5%，主要利多消息是該公司已正式加入參與國防部（Pentagon）機密網路的人工智慧公司名單。
    * **Paramount Skydance：** 上漲 8.3%，接近完成與華納兄弟探索傳媒 (Warner Brothers Discovery) 的合併，是關注點。
* 盤中賣壓明顯的個股則集中在受市場情緒和營收指引影響的科技和消費品公司：
    * **Roblox：** 股價暴跌約 18.3%，主要原因是第一季度日活躍用戶數不及預期，且公司下調了全年預訂量的預測。
    * **Clorox：** 本日跌幅為標準普爾 500 指數最大的跌手，因第三季度有機銷售額未能達到市場共識預期。

Tag: Apple, Oracle, Paramount Skydance, Roblox, Clorox

### English Summary
- The overall market sentiment reflects a strong bullish atmosphere, with major indices like the S&P 500 and NASDAQ setting record highs.
- Market focus is currently concentrated on Big Tech and Information Technology (IT) sectors, which led the market gains.
- Despite continued volatility in geopolitical issues, international oil prices (WTI and Brent) remain elevated above $100 per barrel, yet the stock market appears momentarily disconnected from these macroeconomic risks.
- The S&P 500 recorded another record high, while the NASDAQ Composite and NASDAQ 100 also finished at record levels.
- Apple's stock rose approximately 3.3%, driven by a surprisingly strong Q3 revenue forecast and continued demand for its new product lines, such as the low-cost Neo MacBook and updated iPad Air.
- Oracle gained about 6.5% after officially joining the list of AI companies working with the Pentagon's classified networks.
- Paramount Skydance jumped 8.3% as the company progresses toward finalizing its merger with Warner Brothers Discovery.
- Conversely, Roblox shares plummeted 18.3% after reporting fewer daily active users than expected for Q1 and lowering its full-year booking forecast.
- Clorox was the largest decliner in the S&P 500, falling sharply due to organic sales missing consensus expectations for the third quarter.
- Tech and media sectors were the primary sources of market gains, signaling a strong rotation into growth stocks.
Tag: Apple, Oracle, Paramount Skydance, Roblox, Clorox

Tag: Chevron

### English Summary
- Chevron reported first-quarter earnings per share (EPS) that surpassed analyst expectations, but the CFO emphasized the operational resilience amid global energy market volatility and geopolitical conflicts.
- **Company Operations and Strategy:** The company highlighted its resilient portfolio, noting that U.S. operations achieved a record third consecutive quarter of production exceeding two million barrels of oil equivalent per day (BOE), supported by strong refinery performance.
- Outlook and Guidance: Chevron emphasized that planning is conducted on a long-term cycle basis, rather than reacting to short-term market fluctuations. The company projects production growth of 7% to 10% this year, maintaining strict cost and capital discipline.
- Market Adaptation: To manage risks from areas like the Strait of Hormuz, the company is adjusting product flows to maintain supply chain stability, demonstrating the resilience of its upstream and refining assets.
- Venezuela Focus: Chevron reaffirmed its long-term commitment to Venezuela, leveraging its century-long history and established position in the country, with plans to further increase production capacity and boost Venezuela's physical competitiveness.
