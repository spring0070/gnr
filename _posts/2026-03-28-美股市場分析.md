  2. Bloomberg與ECFC調查顯示，經濟學家預期未來一年通膨將上升，但仍有兩次利率降息空間，且經濟衰退機率從25%升至30%。  
  3. 倉儲債券市場面臨歷史性高供應壓力，美國政府債務需重組約10萬億美元，加上企業發行約2萬億美元，總計達12萬億美元，佔GDP 50%。  

- **市場趨勢**  
  1. 通貨膨脹指標分歧： headline inflation因能源與食品價格上漲而升高，但核心通膨預期仍穩定，市場認為通膨屬暫時性。  
  3. 消費者信心與實際消費數據脫節，航空旅行與零售銷售仍強勁，顯示需求尚未顯著萎縮。  
  4. 債券市場面臨利率與信用 spreads 上升壓力，因供應量龐大且經濟放緩風險增加，投資等級債券收益率吸引人。  
  5. 美聯儲面臨政策兩難：需在控制通膨與維持勞動市場穩定間權衡，若就業數據走弱將增加政策壓力。  

Tag: Apollo, Bloomberg, ECFC, Treasury

### English Summary

- Inflation expectations remain stable, with core inflation uncertain but headline inflation rising due to energy and food. Market and survey data show long-term inflation expectations are stable or declining.  
- Consumer confidence is down, but actual spending (retail, travel, hotels) remains strong, indicating a divergence between sentiment and behavior.  
- Fed faces dilemma between inflation control and labor market risks; rate cuts are still possible but uncertain. Probability of recession increased.  
- Labor market remains strong with low unemployment, but potential softness could impact Fed decisions. Jobless claims are near historical lows.  
- Bond market faces significant supply (14 trillion in investment-grade debt), increasing pressure on rates and credit spreads. Yields and spreads are under upward pressure.  
- High yield and investment grade bonds offer attractive yields amid supply glut, suggesting potential for rate declines and market adjustments.
