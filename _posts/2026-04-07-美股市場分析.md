### 中文摘要

1. **政策調整**：美國政府原計畫於2027年維持Medicare Advantage計畫支付金額不變，但因市場反應劇烈，最終調整為上漲2.48%，較市場預期的1-1.5%顯著優於預期。  
2. **公司動態**：多家保險公司因政策反轉受益，包括United Health、Humana及CVS等，相關股價短線飆升，顯示市場對政策回溫的樂觀情緒。  
3. **市場趨勢**：Medicare Advantage領域持續吸引投資，但未來若政府過度壓縮成本，可能引發企業撤資，形成政策與市場的拉鋸。  
4. **長期不確定性**：主管機關Chris Klomp希望未來逐步降低Medicare Advantage成本，但需平衡企業利潤與政策目標，後續發展仍具不確定性。  

Tag: United Health, Humana, CVS, Medicare Advantage, CMS

### English Summary

- The Trump administration initially planned to freeze Medicare Advantage payments, leading to a market sell-off as companies threatened to exit the space due to reduced revenue.  
- The government reversed course, agreeing to a 2.48% rate increase for 2027, exceeding earlier expectations of 1-1.5%, boosting investor confidence.  
- Companies like UnitedHealth, Humana, and CVS saw immediate gains as they remained in the Medicare Advantage market, benefiting from the rate hike.  
- Market trends show sustained growth for insurers in the sector, with potential long-term gains expected unless cost-cutting measures accelerate too rapidly.  
- CMS official Chris Klomp aims to reduce Medicare Advantage costs, but rapid cuts risk prompting companies to withdraw.  

Tag: [UnitedHealth, Humana, CVS, CMS]
