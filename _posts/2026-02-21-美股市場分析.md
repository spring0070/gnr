# 2. 標題: Nike Races to Capture the Outdoor Market
發布時間: 2026-02-21 00:52:59

點擊下方圖片連結觀看影片:

 [![觀看影片](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/Cb_Qshz5Tzs/sddefault.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Cb_Qshz5Tzs){:target='_blank'}

## 以下是文章的主要重點：

### 中文摘要

1. **Nike復興ACG品牌**：Nike將ACG（All Conditions Gear）從子品牌升格為獨立品牌，定位為專業戶外運動品牌，計畫於2026年冬季奧運會期間正式推出，強調與戶外運動的連結。  
2. **聚焦戶外運動市場**：全球戶外運動參與人數達1.81億（2024年），包含越野跑、攀岩、山地單車等，Nike透過ACG品牌切入市場，主打創新設計與科技應用。  
3. **品牌定位與設計創新**：ACG以「態度」與「風格」為核心，結合戶外運動的極限需求，推出如可調節通風系統的服裝，並強調物理學原理提升散熱效能。  
4. **競爭態勢與市場策略**：面對Patagonia、Hoka、Smartwool等品牌競爭，Nike透過ACG強化「高性價比」與「獨特態度」，並以「持續進攻」為戰略口號，爭取戶外運動族群。  
5. **品牌歷史與挑戰**：ACG起源於1970年代登山者與Nike的合作，後因市場定位混亂一度停產，2010年代重啟但尚未完全站穩腳步，Nike現透過創新重新建立聲譽。  

Tag: Nike, ACG, Patagonia, Hoka, Smartwool

### English Summary

- Nike is reviving its ACG (Advanced Comfort Gear) brand as a dedicated outdoor performance line, positioning it for the 2026 Winter Olympics in Italy.  
- ACG, originally launched in the 1980s, was relaunched in 2010 but faced challenges in gaining traction among outdoor enthusiasts.  
- Nike aims to capitalize on the booming outdoor sports market, with 181 million participants globally in 2024, driven by trail running, ultramarathons, and mountain biking.  
- The brand focuses on trail running, leveraging Nike’s design and tech innovations, such as the inflatable air Milano jacket and radical airflow shirt.  
- Nike’s CEO, Elliott Hill, emphasizes athlete-centric performance and strategic timing for major events like the Olympics, World Cup, and Super Bowl.  
- The company faces competition from brands like Patagonia, Hoka, and Smartwool in the outdoor space, aiming to differentiate through attitude, design, and innovation.  
- Nike’s Chief Design Officer, Martin Lotti, blends high fashion with athletic performance, exemplified by collaborations with athletes like Yannick’s Center.  
- Nike’s strategy includes fostering cross-functional teams and a mantra of “always on offense” to drive fresh, risk-taking approaches in the market.  

Tag: [Nike, Patagonia, Hoka, Smartwool]
