### 中文摘要
*   **市場表現與指標動態：** 在本週收盤當日，股價指數普遍上揚，S&P 500 指數上漲約 0.7%，NASDAQ 100 指數上漲 1.8%。債券方面，兩年期國庫券殖利率有所下降，為投資者提供了喘息空間。
*   **半導體與AI基礎設施成為市場亮點：** 費城半導體指數（Philadelphia semiconductor index）表現亮眼，已連續第七天超越 S&P 500 指數，連續第四週領漲。這表明市場的資金流向正從單純的「能源和工業龍頭股」轉向「AI 基礎設施所需資本支出（Capex）驅動的領域」。
*   **估值與盈利改善支撐：** 本輪上漲的動力核心來自於「盈利改善的故事」（improve earning story）。此前科技股經歷了戰疫導致的修正，使得部分科技股的估值相較於廣泛市場和預期的增長，具有吸引力的買入機會。
*   **股價輪動趨勢變化：** 市場趨勢顯示，這次的科技股復甦不局限於單一的巨頭公司（如 NVIDIA、微軟），而是擴散至了老牌的、傳統的科技產業公司（如德州儀器、英特爾），這代表市場正從純粹的「新經濟增長」敘事，轉向結合了增長與價值維度的「物理資產」環節，包括晶片、資料中心和能源基礎設施。
*   **能源產業的關聯性：** 能源股（Energy stocks）因高度敏感於原油價格，表現仍是市場關注的重點。原油價格（如 Brent）的穩定，對於判斷能源產業的整體定價（re-rated）和未來發展方向具有決定性的參考意義。

Tag: S&P 500, NASDAQ 100, 費城半導體指數, 能源股, AI基礎設施

### English Summary
- Major indices showed solid gains, with the NASDAQ 100 leading the market rally, suggesting strong investor appetite for technology sectors.
- The outperformance of the Philadelphia Semiconductor Index, marking a seventh straight day of gains, confirms a significant and sustained market rotation into AI infrastructure.
- Investor capital appears to be shifting from traditional "energy and industrial leadership stocks" toward sectors driven by capital expenditure (Capex) requirements in AI.
- The market rally is structurally supported by a compelling "improve earning story," suggesting that recent tech corrections have priced some high-quality stocks attractively relative to future growth.
- The trend shows that the current tech recovery is broadening beyond mega-cap leaders (e.g., NVIDIA, Microsoft) to include legacy, essential industrial players like Texas Instruments and Intel.
- This broader rotation indicates a market focus that integrates growth narratives with tangible value, linking modern tech needs to physical assets such as chips, data centers, and energy supplies.
- Falling two-year Treasury yields offer some much-needed liquidity and provide a potential easing of financial pressure for the broader investment community.
- For energy stocks, the sector remains highly sensitive to oil price stability; sustained pricing is key for the industry's overall re-rating and future guidance.
- The general market sentiment reflects a belief that the core technology sector can withstand macroeconomic concerns due to underlying improvements in business spending and earnings.
- The interplay between sustained semiconductor demand and stable oil prices provides a positive backdrop for cyclical sectors, driving integrated investment cycles.

Tag: S&P 500, NASDAQ 100, Philadelphia semiconductor index, Energy stocks, AI infrastructure, Semiconductors

## 以下是文章的主要重點：

分類：未分類

### 中文摘要
*   **市場總體趨勢：** 當日多數主要指數呈現創紀錄的強勁表現。標準普爾 500 指數（S&P 500）朝歷史新高邁進，創下第四個連續上漲的週。
*   **板塊表現：** 資訊科技（Information Technology）和非必需消費品（Consumer Discretionary）是當日表現最佳的板塊，分別上漲了約 2.5% 和 1.4%，推動整體市場上揚。相對地，醫療保健（Health Care）和工業（Industrial）板塊則呈現下跌趨勢。
*   **半導體產業動態：** 晶圓代工與半導體相關股票是當日最大的紅利來源。
    *   Socks（半導體指數）表現極為亮眼，連續第 18 個交易日上漲，在累積期間漲幅約達 50%，創下自 1994 年以來的最長連續漲勢紀錄。
    *   個別晶片股如英特爾（Intel）表現最佳，上漲超過 23%，位列 S&P 500 的第一大漲股。
    *   Arm、AMD 和 Qualcomm 等領頭羊晶片股也呈現大幅上漲，幅度接近或超過 14% 至 15%。
*   **大型權重股表現：** 科技巨頭如 Alphabet（Google）股價也錄得漲幅（約 1.5%）。
*   **主要個股下跌：** 儘管市場普遍看漲，部分公司表現不佳。
    *   Charter Communications 是 S&P 500 的最大跌幅股，跌幅超過 25%，主要因公司公布了不如預期的季度業績，且失去大量住宅客戶。
    *   HCA Healthcare 股價也大幅下跌超過 8.8%，儘管第一季盈利超出分析師預期，但公司卻選擇不提高年度盈利和銷售指引。

Tag: S&P 500, NASDAQ 100, 半導體, Intel, AMD, Charter Communications

### English Summary
- Major stock indices recorded another day of record-setting gains, with the S&P 500 advancing toward a new historical high.
- The S&P 500 marked its fourth consecutive week of gains, reflecting strong overall market sentiment.
- Information Technology and Consumer Discretionary were the top-performing sectors, driving the overall market ascent.
- Semiconductors were the biggest market driver, with the SOX index surging for the 18th consecutive day.
- The SOX rally marked the longest cumulative winning streak for the index since 1994, with gains reaching approximately 50%.
- Individual chipmakers experienced massive gains, leading the S&P 500's biggest gainer list.
- Intel significantly outperformed, rising by over 23% in the session.
- Leading semiconductor firms, including Arm, AMD, and Qualcomm, saw substantial gains, with price increases approaching or exceeding 14–15%.
- Large-cap technology stocks, such as Alphabet (Google), continued to post gains of approximately 1.5%.
- Despite broad market strength, major blue-chip stocks faced declines.
- Charter Communications fell by over 25%, recording its largest single-day decline since 2009 following underwhelming quarterly earnings and customer losses.
- HCA Healthcare dropped by over 8.8%; this decline occurred despite beating Q1 earnings estimates because the company opted not to raise its annual profit or sales guidance.
- The Health Care and Industrial sectors were notable laggards, finishing the day in the red.

Tag: S&P 500, NASDAQ 100, Semiconductors, Intel, AMD, Charter Communications
