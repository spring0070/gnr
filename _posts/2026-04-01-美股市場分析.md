   - 公司宣布將納入Cathie Woods管理的多檔ETF，首度開放零售投資者參與，並提及未來可能上市（IPO）計畫。

2. **市場趨勢**

   - ETF納入私人企業標的，反映資本市場對AI產業的長期看好，並降低投資門檻。  

Tag: Open AI, Anthropic, Google, Cathie Woods, ARC Invest, ETFs

### English Summary

- OpenAI secured a $122 billion funding round, its largest to date, to accelerate research toward AGI (Artificial General Intelligence).  
- The funding targets investments in compute infrastructure and talent to maintain competitiveness against rivals like Anthropic and Google.  
- AI model development cycles have shortened from 6–12 months to every few months, increasing costs and demand for capital.  
- OpenAI plans to include its shares in ETFs managed by Cathie Wood (ARK, Invest), enabling retail investor participation in the company.  
- The fundraising provides flexibility for OpenAI to delay an IPO if needed, while expanding market exposure through retail investors.  
- The company’s valuation has surged to $852 billion following the funding round, reflecting strong market confidence in AI growth.  

Tag: [OpenAI, Anthropic, Google]

## 以下是文章的主要重點：

### 中文摘要

- **AI對就業市場衝擊**：AI技術加速取代人力，部分科技公司已實現「1人取代7人」的效率，預期未來3-5年將持續影響就業結構，尤其在法律、金融等知識密集產業。  
- **軟體公司表現分化**：Microsoft、Gemini等軟體股因AI應用進展受挫，股價下挫，而Apple跌幅較輕，反映市場對AI長期影響的分歧觀點。  
- **市場趨勢與資本流動**：被動投資（如SP500指數基金）扭曲市場，導致信用利差擴大風險，建議透過ETF（如HYG、LQD）做保護性操作，以對冲市場崩盤風險。  
- **ETF策略與風險管理**：採用「保護性價差」（Put Spreads）策略，鎖定ETF下檔風險，因市場流動性可能因AI衝擊而惡化，尤其信用市場易遭擠兌。  
- **AI企業IPO預期**：SpaceX擬以1.75兆美元上市，Open AI與Anthropic亦被討論，但市場對高估值IPO反應保守，建議觀望而非直接做多或做空。  

Tag: Microsoft, Gemini, Apple, Nvidia, Open AI, Anthropic, SpaceX, HYG, LQD

### English Summary

- AI is reshaping the job market, with leading tech firms using it to replace up to 7 colleagues, raising concerns about workforce shrinkage and long-term economic impact.  
- Software companies like Microsoft (down 23%) and Apple (down 6.5%) are under pressure, while Gemini faces scrutiny despite having a real LLM.  
- Passive investing is criticized for distorting markets, with Mike Green’s theory linking it to potential market crashes exacerbated by AI-driven disruptions.  
- Credit spreads are expected to widen, prompting strategies like long-dated put spreads on ETFs (e.g., HYG, LQD) to hedge against market volatility.  
- The "AI apocalypse" scenario could trigger a global financial crisis-like fallout, faster than the 2008 GFC due to accelerated information flow.  
- Big tech IPOs like SpaceX (targeting $1.75T) and OpenAI/Anthropic are speculated to face challenges due to market saturation and retail investor influence.  
- Portfolio strategies focus on momentum-driven investments in S&P 500 constituents, with Palantir as a past success example.  

Tag: [Microsoft, Gemini, Apple, SpaceX, OpenAI, Anthropic, Palantir, HYG, LQD]
