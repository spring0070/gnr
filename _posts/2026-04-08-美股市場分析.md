- **軟體業危機預警：** 直接貸款領域，特別是針對軟體產業，預期面臨嚴重的崩盤。由於過去依賴過高的年經常性收入（ARR）估值，貸款槓桿率過高（8-10倍），預計在未來几年內將面臨大量違約，且預估回收率極低。
- **資金流向與優勢領域：** 投資者正避開軟體類貸款，轉向「機會型信貸」，專注於廣泛經濟體內的分散性（dislocation）和困境（distress）貸款解決方案。
- **實物資產的吸引力：** 獲取擔保品為實體硬資產（如基礎設施、設備）的資產基礎貸款（Asset-based Lending）更受青睞，因為其抵押品提供的保護和回收率更穩定，特別是在美國再工業化浪潮帶動的領域。
- **市場參與動態：** 大型機構（如Blackstone）正積極投入數十億美元進行機會型信貸，顯示市場正從泡沫破裂的特定領域，轉向更廣泛、更有基礎保護性的信貸機會。
Tag: Blackstone, Marathon

### English Summary
- **Market Divergence in Private Credit:** The private credit space is showing a structural shift away from highly leveraged Direct Lending towards Opportunistic Credit.
- **Software Sector Warning:** The direct lending segment, particularly in software, is facing a significant correction. Loans backed by excessive leverage (8-10x) derived from inflated ARR valuations are expected to face mass defaults and very low recovery rates (projected at 0-30 cents on a dollar).
- **Shift to Opportunity Credit:** Capital is moving into Opportunity Credit, focusing on distressed situations and capital solutions across diverse, non-software sectors.
- **Preference for Hard Assets:** Asset-based lending, which uses physical hard assets (like infrastructure or equipment) as collateral, is preferred due to superior protection and more stable recovery rates, especially amid reindustrialization trends.
- **Market Flow:** Major institutional players (like Blackstone) are actively deploying large capital sums into distressed opportunities, signaling a move toward broader, more fundamentally sound credit plays.
Tag: Blackstone, Marathon
