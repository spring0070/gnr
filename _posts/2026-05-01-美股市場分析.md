*   **企業的資金籌措策略：** 在當前的環境下，企業為持續擴大AI相關資本基礎設施，比起稀釋股權更傾向於透過低成本的借貸方式來籌集營運資金，顯示出資本市場對增長敘事的高度信任。

### English Summary
- The market exhibits a strong upward momentum, with both the S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 reaching new record highs, signaling a significant shift to a risk-on sentiment.
- The primary driving force behind the current rally is the anticipated massive capital expenditure (CAPEX) fueled by the advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AI).
- Corporate earnings projections and economic relief have underpinned the gains, with CAPEX identified as the main contributor to Q1 adjusted GDP growth.
- Major U.S. technology corporations are planning to invest an estimated $725 billion in CAPEX this year, significantly surpassing prior investment models.
- Investment capital is shifting from the companies spending the money (capex spenders) to the tech mega-caps that stand to benefit from this expenditure.
- Consequently, AI-exposed tech leaders, such as Nvidia, Microsoft, and Amazon, are showing superior performance compared to traditional industries.
- Companies are increasingly favoring low-cost borrowing over equity issuance to fund their expanding AI-related infrastructure, indicating high confidence in the market's growth narrative.
- Despite elevated geopolitical risks, such as the situation in the Strait of Hormuz, investors are demonstrating a trend of "looking through the event."
- The market's focus remains heavily weighted on the potential upward trajectory and benefits offered by AI, outweighing short-term concerns over consumer spending drag.
- The outperformance of big tech has characterized the recent market action, differentiating it from the broader market indices and equal-weighted metrics.
- On the macro level, the market appears to be pricing in a resolution to global disruptions, allowing positive AI potential to overshadow temporary economic headwinds.
