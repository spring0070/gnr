# 1. 標題: SpaceX Said to Target Confidential IPO Filing as Soon as March
發布時間: 2026-02-28 05:07:59

 [![觀看影片](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/S_Jlhjf1_xI/sddefault.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S_Jlhjf1_xI){:target='_blank'}

## 以下是文章的主要重點：

### 中文摘要

- **SpaceX IPO動態**：SpaceX計畫於3月提交保密IPO文件，目標在6月中期至晚期上市，可能成為美國第六大上市公司，估值或突破1.75萬億美元（此前估計為1.25萬億美元）。  
- **市場趨勢**：此IPO規模龐大，引發市場對消化能力的討論，投資者可能需出售現有資產（如咖啡店股份）以籌資參與IPO。  
- **競爭影響**：若SpaceX成功上市，可能加速OpenAI與Anthropic等同屬千億美元級公司的上市計畫，並可能引發其他企業在6月爭取上市資格。  
- **SEC審查**：IPO進程需經歷SEC的往返審查，可能影響上市時間表。  

Tag: SpaceX, OpenAI, Anthropic

### English Summary

- SpaceX is preparing for an IPO, targeting a confidential filing in March with potential public listing by mid-to-late June.  
- The IPO could position SpaceX as a top-tier company, potentially valued over $1.75 trillion post-merger with XAI, surpassing OpenAI and Anthropic in valuation.  
- Market concerns include digesting multiple large IPOs (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) and liquidity challenges, though investors may sell existing holdings to fund new investments.  
- Historical precedents, like Alibaba’s IPO, suggest capital availability exists for massive offerings, though knock-on effects like market saturation remain uncertain.  
Tag: SpaceX, XAI, OpenAI, Anthropic, Alibaba
