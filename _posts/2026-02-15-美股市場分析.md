### 中文摘要

1. **公司動態**  
   - 創立音樂經紀公司「Violator」，成功管理多位知名藝人如LL Cool J、50 Cent、Mariah Carey等。  
   - 從音樂產業轉型至電視製作，擔任《Love & Hip Hop》節目製作人，開創黑人娛樂真人秀先河。  
   - 成立自有製片公司，拓展至電影與劇本製作領域，提供非裔女性職業發展機會。  

2. **市場趨勢**  
   - 嘻哈文化於1990年代成為主流，衍生出「黑人文化」為核心的生活方式與時尚趨勢。  
   - 真人秀節目興起，以「揭開幕後」形式呈現藝人關係與生活，成為跨文化內容創作模板。  
   - 非裔族群內容創作逐漸被主流市場接受，但後製與執行層面仍由非黑人主導，代表性不足。

### English Summary

- **Company News**:  
  - Interviewee managed prominent artists via **Violator**, a successful music management company.  
  - Transitioned from music management to producing reality TV shows like *Love & Hip Hop*, which became a template for other reality formats.  
  - Founded a production company to expand into scripted content and film.  

- **Market Trends**:  
  - Hip hop culture’s rise to mainstream popularity in the 1990s transformed it into a global lifestyle and pop culture phenomenon.  
  - Reality TV evolved to showcase behind-the-scenes aspects of celebrity lifestyles, with *Love & Hip Hop* highlighting relationships and struggles within the industry.  
  - Increasing demand for diverse representation in media, though challenges remain in behind-the-scenes roles and executive leadership.
